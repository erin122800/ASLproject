Events

Deaf Community Events Near Long Island and NYC!

Deaf Is Not A Dirty Word

This event is an interview with Deaf residents of New York, where they get asked questions about their daily life, jobs, and how being Deaf affects their opportunities. The interview will be held in ASL with an interpreter for speaking people in the audience. The interview is on April 3rd at 7:30 pm at The Tank. Tickets are $10 and both speaking and Deaf people are welcome.


New York Deaf Theatre Community Mixer

The New York Deaf Theatre organization is holding a Spring Teaser Community Mixer! Join them on March 28th from 7:00pm to 10:00 pm for a chance to meet the staff from the organization and Deaf talent from past a present productions. There will also be an opportunity to win an assortment of prizes from their raffle! Come to Stich, 247 West 37th Street in New York City.

NYC Deaf Sports Club

The New York City Deaf Sports Club welcomes everyone to play, including Deaf, Hard of Hearing, CODA, interpreters, and students who learn sign language. On April 1st at 7:30pm, the organization is hosting a Deaf/Hard of Hearing Basketball Night. The game is between Brooklyn and Milwaukee at Atlantic Avenue Barclays Center Station in Brooklyn. Remember, teamwork makes the dream work!
