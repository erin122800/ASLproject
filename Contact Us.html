About Us


This website was made by two students, Paige Manini and Erin Laxton, to inform the public on the distinction between people with hearing loss who are involved in the Deaf community and those who are not. The homepage offers some education on Deaf pride and those who prefer an oralistic approach to their affliction. We wanted to use the Internet to form a greater outreach to the Deaf community and use this as an opportunity to promote events hosted by the community. On this page, you also have the option to send information on some local events for us to screen and add to the website! .
